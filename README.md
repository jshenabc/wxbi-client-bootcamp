# IBM watsonx BI Client Bootcamp

## Objectives

- Deepen your understanding of the  watsonx BI product capabilities that can help answer your business questions in natural language and provide AI power business insights using the enterprise data.

- Hands-on experience in configuring and deploying business metrics to quickly enable your business leaders and business analysts with a rich AI powered conversational experience to interact with the enterprise data using watsonx BI.

- Learn ways to maximize your Cognos Analytics investments (FM models and Metrics) to provide powerful conversational experience and AI based business insights to your business users.
